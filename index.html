Impact of Singapore's Internet Companies on HDB Resale Prices

Hypothesis

Since 2017, tech companies like Shopee and TikTok have hired large numbers of foreign software engineers. Many obtain PR status within 1–2 years and become eligible to purchase HDB flats or private property. While both Queenstown and Bukit Merah had similar resale prices in 2017, Queenstown's quieter environment and proximity to the MRT green line may make it more attractive to tech workers. We hypothesise that this led to a sharper price increase in Queenstown over time.

Findings

Despite Bukit Merah being geographically closer to the CBD and Chinatown, Queenstown experienced higher resale price growth from 2017 to 2025. This supports the hypothesis that lifestyle preferences—such as quieter surroundings and direct green line MRT access—can influence settlement patterns among foreign tech workers and, in turn, shape housing demand and pricing.
